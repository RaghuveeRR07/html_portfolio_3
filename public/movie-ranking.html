The Best Movies According To RV

My top 3 movies of all time

Zindagi Na Milegi dobara

A hilarious and fun story of 3 friends on their vacation to Spain. It gives life lessons with a touch of humour in every scene.

3 idiots

This is a movie about Indian engineering students and their college life. THe film portrays reality of indian engineers and the education system as well.

Death Note

My first ever anime. I loved the character L and really enjoyed the thriller mind fights between light Yagami and L.
